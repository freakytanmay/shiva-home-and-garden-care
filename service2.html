Vertical green garden wall

  • Home
  • services
  • Vertical green garden wall

Shiva Home & Garden Care standardizes work going on a vertical green garden wall. Which is based on natural plants, organic fertilizer & weather depending plants. Fully designable & attractive look.

We work on a fully systematic approach with a perfect size of the planter which cares & helps the plant. Plants are used based on sun direction & region location. All the work maintains by a water irrigation dripper system, so zero maintenance walls are made with natural plants.

How Shiva’s team works:

In this service, we have set an aluminum structure for back support to vertical wall containers as per dimension. To the structure support, we cladding the containers (planters). If are you get a fully irrigated wall system then we will provide an irrigation system to each container for water curing. After that in the last stage, we set a plant as per design & planted a species. In last cure the wall with fully automatic irrigation system & handover to you.

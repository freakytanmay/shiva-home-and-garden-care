Shiva Home & Garden Care

Shiva Home & Garden Care was incepted in the India in 2020, Our core business activities are the Land developing with base of architecture Landscaping & Garden consulting on (hard scaping &soft scaping). We provide major 3 core services which is Planning – Designing, Execution – Installation, Maintenance – Care services.

Landscaping is both a science and an art, and requires good observation and design skills. Shiva home & Garden care team understands the elements of nature and construction and blends them accordingly.

We are a company totally dedicated to environmental ethos, and shall ensure the continuation of a safe, clean and healthy ecosystem. We also have solid business processes that are geared towards a greener environment.
